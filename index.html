Renewable Energy in India

Solar parks, wind farms, micro-hydro and biomass solutions are reshaping energy systems while reducing pollution.

  • Solar: Rooftop & utility-scale PV reduce coal dependence.
  • Wind: Offshore and onshore wind power growing rapidly.
  • Storage: Batteries and smart grids stabilise renewables.
Citizen tips
  • Use LED lights and energy-star appliances.
  • Install rooftop solar if possible.
  • Reduce standby power use (unplug chargers).

Biodiversity & Conservation

India is megadiverse — protecting habitats preserves livelihoods and culture. Programs like Project Tiger and community forestry work at local scales.
